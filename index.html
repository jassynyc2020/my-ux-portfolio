Case Studies

MySensAR

Have you ever been sick or hurt while having no health insurance and no money to see a doctor? Or been fearful or reluctant to see a doctor?

Problem Statement

Giving people more control over their health with self-education and self-diagnosis by integrating augmented reality to pinpoint the exact location of their symptoms. It saves money, time and worries over going to a doctor's office, especially for those who are reluctant to see a doctor or need to save their money.
